Divorce Appraisals

Finalizing a divorce involves many decisions, including "Who gets the house"? There are generally two options regarding the house - it can be sold and the proceeds divided, or one party can "buy out" the other. In either case, one or both parties should order an appraisal of the residence. Divorce appraisals require a well supported, professional appraisal that will hold up in court. When you order an appraisal from me, you are assured that you will get the best in professional service, courtesy, and the highest quality appraisal. We also know how to handle the sensitive needs of a divorce situation as well as testifying as an expert witness.

Any Residential Appraisals

Residential appraisals are professional assessments of a home's market value conducted by certified appraisers. This service is essential for homeowners, buyers, sellers, and lenders, providing an impartial evaluation that factors in the property's location, condition, size, and comparable sales within the vicinity. Appraisals are crucial for mortgage lending, property insurance, and taxation purposes, ensuring that all parties have an accurate understanding of the property's value for sales, refinancing, or settling estates.
